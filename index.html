Who We Are

Oak City Advisors was founded on the principle that day-to-day operations within the staffing industries shouldn't be a full-time headache. Dan Mastropolo, a top 100 Global Staffing leader leading the industry in technology and innovation, set out to fix this issue and make the world of automation more accessible to staffing owners across the country. With a proven track record of simplifying complicated workflows, we look forward to helping you solve yours.
